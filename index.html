How to make pizza

Everyone loves pizza. Who doesn't? Here is how to make a quick home made pizza.

ingredients/preperation

  • Pizza base
  • Tomato sauce
  • Grated cheddar and mozzarella cheese
  • Any other toppings of your choice
  • An oven that has been preheated to 180 degrees, or gas mark 4

Recipe

  1. Take your pizza base and set it onto a flat surface
  2. Evenly spread a thin layer tomato sauce around the pizza base, making sure that all of the base is covered by it
  3. Next, sprinkle a mixture of the grated cheese on top of the pizza base and again make sure that it is evenly spread out
  4. Now, add your toppings of choice
  5. Put the pizza into the oven for 10 minutes, or until the pizza is golden-brown
  6. Enjoy!
